# Kanban Boards

Organize your projects with flexible Kanban boards that adapt to your workflow.

## Multi-Board Support

Create separate boards for different projects, teams, or workflows:

- **Project Isolation**: Each board is completely independent with its own lanes and notes
- **Easy Switching**: Navigate between boards instantly with the board selector
- **Unlimited Boards**: Create as many boards as you need for different purposes
- **Persistent State**: Each board maintains its own layout, color scheme, and settings

## Board Management

### Creating Boards

1. Click the "+" button in the board selector
2. Enter a descriptive name for your board
3. Choose initial settings (optional)
4. Start adding lanes and notes

### Board Features

- **Color Coding**: Assign unique color schemes to visually distinguish boards
- **Easy Switching**: Navigate between boards using the board selector dropdown


## Board Organization

### Lane Management

- **Custom Lanes**: Create lanes that match your workflow (Backlog, In Progress, Review, Done)
- **Hidden Lanes**: Hide lanes from view while preserving their content, useful for completed work or reference lanes
- **Drag & Drop**: Move notes between lanes to track progress
- **Lane Ordering**: Arrange lanes in the order that makes sense for your workflow
- **Unlimited Lanes**: Create as many lanes as needed for complex workflows

## Use Cases

### Software Development

- **Sprint Planning**: Organize user stories and tasks for upcoming sprints
- **Bug Tracking**: Manage bug reports and their resolution status
- **Feature Development**: Track feature progress from idea to deployment
- **Code Review**: Organize pull requests and review tasks

### Content Creation

- **Blog Posts**: Track articles from idea to publication
- **Video Production**: Manage video creation workflow
- **Social Media**: Plan and schedule content across platforms
- **Design Projects**: Organize design assets and approval stages

### Personal Productivity

- **Task Management**: Organize personal to-dos and projects
- **Goal Tracking**: Break down large goals into actionable steps
- **Habit Building**: Track daily habits and routines
- **Learning Plans**: Organize educational goals and progress
